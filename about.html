BOYNEXTDOOR is a 6-member South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment, consisting of Jaehyun, Sungho, Riwoo, Taesan, Leehan, and Woonhak. They debuted on May 30, 2023, with their first single album, WHO!. They made their Japanese debut on July 10, 2024. They recently won "Favorite Male Group" and the 2025 MAMA Awards.
